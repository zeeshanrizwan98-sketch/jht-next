### Introduction to External Wall Insulation (EWI) in England

External Wall Insulation (EWI) is an effective way to reduce heat loss in homes across England, especially for properties with solid walls or those lacking cavity wall insulation. This process involves attaching a layer of insulating material to the exterior of your property, followed by a protective render or cladding.

By leveraging **government-backed schemes like ECO4** (Energy Company Obligation) and **GBIS** (Great British Insulation Scheme), we help you lower heating costs and improve energy efficiency.

As energy costs in England continue to rise, EWI is an excellent way to save money, stay comfortable, and contribute to environmental sustainability.

### What Is External Wall Insulation (EWI)?

External Wall Insulation involves applying a thermal barrier to the outside of your building’s exterior walls. This method does not reduce your home’s internal living space, making it a popular choice for homes in England.

**Key Components of EWI Systems**:

1. **Insulation Boards**: Materials like expanded polystyrene (EPS), mineral wool, or phenolic resin boards trap heat and prevent it from escaping.
2. **Adhesive**: Strong adhesives are used to attach the boards to the walls.
3. **Reinforcement Mesh**: Embedded in a base coat to increase durability and crack resistance.
4. **Render or Cladding**: Protects the insulation and enhances the appearance of your home.

### Types of Insulating Materials for Homes in England

#### 1\. Expanded Polystyrene (EPS)

- **Advantages**: Lightweight, affordable, and easy to install.
- **Drawbacks**: Less fire-resistant compared to other materials.

#### 2\. Mineral Wool

- **Advantages**: Excellent thermal, acoustic, and fire-resistant properties.
- **Drawbacks**: Heavier and slightly more expensive than EPS.

#### 3\. Phenolic Resin Boards

- **Advantages**: Superior thermal performance in thinner layers.
- **Drawbacks**: Higher upfront cost.

#### 4\. Rigid Foam Boards (Polyurethane or PIR)

- **Advantages**: Moisture-resistant and effective for insulation.
- **Drawbacks**: More expensive than EPS.

### The External Wall Insulation Process

At **JHT Energy Services**, we follow a systematic approach to ensure professional and efficient installation of EWI for homeowners in England:

#### Step 1: Assessment & Survey

Our qualified surveyors assess your property’s suitability for EWI. We evaluate the wall condition, identify funding opportunities through ECO4 and GBIS, and provide a free quote.
