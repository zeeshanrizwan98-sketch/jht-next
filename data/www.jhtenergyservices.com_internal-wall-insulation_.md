#### **Internal Wall Insulation Grants Explained**

The Great British Insulation Scheme (GBIS) and Energy Company Obligation (ECO4) are government-backed initiatives to help homeowners and renters improve their energy efficiency. Here’s how they work:

- **GBIS** offers a single insulation upgrade per qualifying household based on a property assessment to maximize energy savings.
- **ECO4** provides a holistic approach, offering a range of energy-efficiency upgrades, including internal wall insulation, heating system improvements, and more.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

To qualify for these grants, you must meet one or more of the following conditions:

- **Low-Income Households**: An annual gross income below £31,000, or living in social housing and vulnerable to cold conditions.
- **EPC Rating**: Homes with an Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) rating of D or below (D-G for homeowners; D-E for private renters).
- **Government Benefits**: Recipients of income-based benefits, including Universal Credit, Pension Credit, and others.

**Important Note:** Grants require professional installation by certified installers. DIY installations are not eligible.

These grants can provide free or low-cost internal wall insulation to qualifying households.
