#### Who Can Qualify for ECO4 and GBIS in England?

To qualify for **ECO4** or **GBIS**, you must meet specific criteria:

**ECO4 Eligibility**:

- Non-condensing boiler installed before 2005.
- Broken boiler that cannot be repaired.
- Receipt of qualifying government benefits.

**GBIS Eligibility**:

- Your property in **England** needs loft or cavity wall insulation.
- Up-to-date Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) is required.

Eligibility also depends on the type of property and its current energy performance rating.
