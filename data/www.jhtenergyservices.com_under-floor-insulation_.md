### How Does Underfloor Insulation Work?

Underfloor insulation involves placing insulating materials beneath your floorboards to trap heat and block drafts. This is particularly effective for homes with suspended timber floors, which are common in England’s older properties.

### Government Support for Insulation in England

We understand that the cost of insulation can be a concern for homeowners, which is why we help you take advantage of government schemes like **ECO4** (Energy Company Obligation) and **GBIS** (Great British Insulation Scheme).

These programs are designed to make energy efficiency upgrades more accessible and affordable for households across England.

#### What Is ECO4?

ECO4 is a government initiative that provides funding for energy efficiency improvements, including underfloor insulation. If you meet the eligibility criteria, you could receive a grant that covers part or all of your insulation costs.

#### What Is GBIS?

GBIS is another government scheme aimed at improving the insulation of homes in England. It offers financial support to help homeowners and tenants install high-quality insulation solutions.
