### Heat Pump Installation

Heat pump installation involves setting up a system that uses electricity to transfer heat from one location to another. These systems are a popular choice for both residential and commercial properties as they are energy-efficient and can be used for both heating and cooling.

![External Wall Insulation](https://www.jhtenergyservices.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/istockphoto-1049230414-612x612-1.jpg)

### External Wall Insulation

External wall insulation is a process in which insulation material is applied to the exterior walls of a building. This type of insulation helps to improve the energy efficiency of the building by reducing heat loss through the walls.

![Boiler Installation](https://www.jhtenergyservices.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/boiler-repair-e1663251449775.jpg)

### Boiler Installation

Boiler installation involves setting up a system that heats water or generates steam for use in homes and businesses. Boilers can be fueled by natural gas, oil, or electricity, and are an important part of many heating, hot water, and industrial processes.

![](https://www.jhtenergyservices.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/energy-assessment-service.webp)

### Energy Assessments

Energy assessments are a service that helps identify ways to reduce energy consumption and save money. Energy assessments can help lower energy bills and reduce environmental impact.

![underfloor-insulation](https://www.jhtenergyservices.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/underfloor-insulation.jpg)

### Under Floor Insulation

Underfloor insulation is a service that involves installing insulation beneath the floorboards of a building to improve energy efficiency. Underfloor insulation helps reduce energy bills and increases comfort by keeping the building warmer in winter and cooler in summer.

![Internal Wall Insulation](https://www.jhtenergyservices.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/Internal-wall-insulation.jpg)

### Internal Wall Installation

Internal wall installation is a service that involves adding new walls within an existing structure. Internal walls can be made of a variety of materials, including drywall, plaster, or wood, and are typically installed by a team of skilled contractors.

![](https://www.jhtenergyservices.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/loft-insulation-service.webp)

### Loft Insulation

Loft insulation is a service that involves installing insulation in the attic or loft space of a building to improve energy efficiency. There are several types of materials that can be used for loft insulation, including fiberglass, cellulose, and spray foam.
